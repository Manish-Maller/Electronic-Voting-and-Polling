Welcome to Exit Poll Analysis

“Exit Poll Analysis” is a Digital Voting Poll Analysis Project. In this System People who have citizenship of a particular Country and Whose age is above 18 years of any gender can give his/her vote online without going to any physical polling station. This Project Deals with the Immediate Result of the Election.

In “Exit Poll Analysis” a Voter can use his/her voting right online without any difficulty. He\She can Cast their Vote by their Unique Voter ID Number. There is a Database which is maintained by the Election Commission of India in which all the names of the Voter with complete information is stored The System Administrator (admin) Validates these Voter ID Number and Register the Votes in the Database.

Admin can Calculate the Total Number of Votes and to their Respective Parties Votes. Analysing these Votes Counting and Their Interest to the Parties. The System Administrator (admin) Declares the Result of the Election..
